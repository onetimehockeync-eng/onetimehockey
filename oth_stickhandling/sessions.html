Session Plans

Walk In.
Know the Plan.

Four structured 30–45 minute sessions for the Montreal Canadiens lane. Each has a warm-up, skill block, trick highlight, and a finish that closes on a confidence moment.

Run sessions in order on your first pass through the program. On repeat sessions, use whichever session matches your current training focus. Every session starts with foundation warm-up — no exceptions.

Session tip

Set a timer on your phone for each block. When it goes off, move on — even if you didn't hit the reps target. Session discipline beats perfection in any single drill.

Trick time

Every session ends on a trick highlight. Even if you don't land it clean, end the session working on it. The session closes on confidence — not frustration.

The standard

Slow down before you look down. Every time you look at the puck, you are reinforcing a habit that keeps you at your current level. Speed comes after eyes-up — not before.
