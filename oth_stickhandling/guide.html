Foundation — 9 Drills
IDDrillTagDurationDescriptionCoach Tip
F1Around the Worldfoundation3x each dir / 3 setsFull circle around body. Top hand manipulates blade — bottom hand supports.The stick twists in your hands throughout. Keep it controlled and smooth.
F2Side to Side — Full Reachfoundation30 sec / 3 setsReach puck far outside each foot.Work on being smooth. Head Up.
F3Toe Drag — Stationaryfoundation30 sec / 3 setsUse the toe of blade to pull the puck back — pull across to the backhand.Move lateral — not forward. Defender is in front of you.
F4Pull to Skatefoundation10 reps / 3 sets FH&BHPull puck to skate toe and kick back. Forehand and backhand.Add quick handles between reps. Move your feet. Stay loose.
F5Quick Handlesfoundation30 sec / 5 setsRapid micro-taps. Top hand rotates — don't chop. 4–6 inches each side.This is all about the top hand. Don't tighten your shoulder. Stay loose.
F6Top-Hand Onlyconditioning30 sec / 3 setsRemove bottom hand. Dribble with top hand only. 30 continuous seconds.If forearm burns by 30 sec — add wrist/forearm training off ice.
F7Danglesfoundation30 sec / 4 setsTwo pucks (small objects) 18" apart. Figure-8 both directions. Progress to 12".Stay loose. Find a rhythm. Move your feet.
F8Minefield — Eyes Upgame30 sec / 3 setsPlace 6–8 small objects in a random pattern.Your brain knows where the obstacles are. Trust yourself. Eyes up.
F8Flowstylegame30 sec / 3 setsStickhandle freely with no restrictions, eyes up the whole time.The brain runs the hands. Let your hands go. Stream of consciousness stickhandling..
Advanced — 6 Drills
IDDrillTagDurationDescriptionCoach Tip
A1Shot Fake — FH to BHgame10 reps / 3 setsLoad fake shot → kill puck dead → backhand drag across → finish with forward step.Sell the shot. If defender doesn't believe you'll shoot, they won't bite.
A2Between-the-Legsgame10 reps / 3 sets each4 variations: front leg forward, front leg back, off skate FH, off skate BH.Bend down to widen space between legs. Head up.
A3Jump Deke — Chip Over Stickgame3 reps / 3 sets FH&BHLay stick on ice. Chip puck 3–6 inches over it — forehand and backhand.Use peripherals. 3–6 inches is enough. Move your feet.
A4Wall Ball — Heads Upgame1 min / 4 setsPass and receive off wall — FH and BH — head up the entire time. Cushion, no stab.Firm passes, soft hands on receive. Find a corner for angled passes.
A5Wall Ball — Speedgame30 sec / 3 sets5 feet from wall. One-touch passing as fast as possible. Find rhythm then push harder.Head up. Aim for a spot and hit it. Challenge yourself each set.
A6Ball Juggletrick50+ repsJuggle a tennis ball with your stick blade. 50+ consecutive reps. Beat your record.Concentrate. Relax your shoulders. The tennis ball demands greater blade sensitivity.
Tricks — 9 Drills
IDDrillTagDurationDescriptionCoach Tip
T1Pancake Pickuptrick10 pickupsSlide blade under puck and scoop. Hold on blade. Gently return.Scoop and hold IS the skill. Be automatic before juggling.
T2Toe Pickuptrick10 pickupsToe on far edge — bottom hand thumb levers blade under. Pop up.Easier while moving. Practice stationary until wrist motion is automatic.
T3Scoop Pickuptrick10 pickupsToe drag → drop hands → heel contact → slice under. Momentum carries puck onto blade.Easier on ice with momentum. Work stationary until blade angle is automatic.
T4Forehand Juggletrick10 jugglesPickup → 1/2 revolution → catch on forehand blade. 10 clean reps.Bend knees for greater catch control. Eye on rotation — not blade.
T5Forehand–Backhand Juggletrick10 juggles FH&BHToss from FH → catch on BH → flip back to FH. Alternating.Rotate hands early to prepare for catch — don't wait until the puck is falling.
T6High Juggletrick5 jugglesFull revolution toss — catch on forehand. Track rotation all the way down.Eye on the puck's spin — not its height. Soft hands on catch.
T7Stick Walk Juggletrick5 jugglesPickup → walk to heel with 1/2 rev → launch straight up → catch on blade.Practice heel-to-blade transition separately before connecting full pattern.
T8Zoro — Airtrick5 repsPickup → roll bottom hand over top → puck spins around blade → return. 5 reps.Find consistent rhythm before adding speed. Wrist coordination — don't muscle it.
T9Windmilltrick5 repsPickup → bottom hand up and around → centripetal force holds puck → 5 complete rotations.Consistent speed is the entire skill. Hesitation drops the puck.
